🚀 Start Tour
On the day of the tour, the buyer navigates to the Deals / Tours tab and taps the property. When the buyer is at the property and ready to begin, they tap Start Tour. This moves the deal to the "Tour Started" stage.

After the tour is marked complete, the buyer goes through a 3-step feedback flow: first deciding whether to consider the property further, then rating it, then leaving notes and photos. All steps can be skipped.

Contract & Closing
Select vendors (attorney, lender, inspector) → dealsheet → contract → mortgage → closing
Once the offer is accepted and the buyer taps "Select Vendors", they choose their attorney, lender, and home inspector to begin the contract and closing process.

The buyer selects three vendors in sequence: Attorney (required) → Lender (required) → Home Inspector (if needed). All are drawn from the Nestly-preferred network. After selection, the deal advances to the Dealsheet stage.

After all vendors are selected, the agent prepares a dealsheet summarising all agreed terms — price, contingencies, closing date, and parties involved. Both buyer and agent review and sign off before the formal contract is drafted.

Dealsheet Review

✍️ Contract Required

Sign Contract
  • Full contract document rendered in-app (PDF viewer)

Screens to be finalised.
🏦 Mortgage Processing If Needed
If the buyer is financing via mortgage, the selected lender is notified and the mortgage application process begins in parallel with the contract stage. Cash buyers skip this step entirely.
Contract — Step 3 (if financed)
Mortgage Application
  • Lender receives buyer's details and deal summary automatically
  • Buyer is notified of mortgage application status in-app
  • Status updates: Applied → Under Review → Pre-Approved → Approved
  • Buyer may need to upload supporting documents (pay stubs, bank statements)
Screens to be finalised. Once mortgage is approved (or for cash buyers, once contract is signed), the deal advances to Closing.
🏁 Closing Final Step
The final stage of the deal. All parties have signed the contract and (if applicable) the mortgage is approved. The buyer completes the closing process and receives the keys.
Contract — Step 4
Closing
  • Final walkthrough of the property confirmed
  • Closing disclosure reviewed — final costs, fees, credits
  • Buyer signs all closing documents digitally
  • Funds transferred (down payment + closing costs)
  • Title transferred and deed recorded
  • CTA: Complete Closing — marks the deal as fully closed
Transaction bar completes all stages: Tour ✓ → Offer ✓ → Contract ✓ → Closing ✓

Buyer receives a congratulatory confirmation and the property is marked as Sold in their account.
